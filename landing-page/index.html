A Powerful Multi-Purpose Recruitment Agency Theme

Check Out These Awesome Features

Easily Create your own Recruitment Website

With Recruitment Agency theme, you can easily set up your own recruitment agency website with no coding required! We’ve done the work for you.

Apply For Multiple Jobs With One Click!

A unique feature in Recruitment Agency theme is the one-click application of jobs in the basket. You can add multiple jobs in the basket and providing the candidate has uploaded their CV then they can simply apply for all the jobs in their basket at once.

Candidate Profile Interface

The candidate profile page contains everything a candidate needs to get applying for jobs. From here they can edit account details, view their application history, set up job alerts and more! All custom designed with the candidates in mind!

Application Status’

View the status of your job applications within the application history tab. When the employer approves or rejects your application then it will be shown in your application history.

Job Basket

Add multiple jobs to your job basket and providing you have uploaded your CV then you can apply to them all with one click. A truely unique feature not found in other recruitment themes on ThemeForest.

Job Alerts

Set custom alerts which email you when jobs posted in the critera you’re interested in. Choose keywords within the job sectors, location and employment types of your choice.
